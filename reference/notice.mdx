`appleai.codes` is a third-party relay layer. It is not the model vendor.

## What that means

- Upstream model behavior, limits, and outages still come from the original vendor
- Your organization should review data handling before you route sensitive prompts or source code
- Vendor support teams may ask you to reproduce issues against the direct upstream API

## Recommended operating posture

1. Validate a routed model with small prompts first
2. Keep a direct upstream fallback for critical workflows
3. Re-test after major CLI or editor-agent updates

Use the relay when it improves routing, cost control, or tool compatibility. Do not treat it as a zero-risk drop-in replacement for every upstream provider workflow.
